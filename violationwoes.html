Violation Woes

"Discipline starts not with a 'D', but with a queue."

In the model with social distancing, there was a significant assumption that everyone follows social distancing strictly. However, in reality, this does not occur. The simulation below shows what happens if 8% of the population does not adhere to the lockdown.

Some not following social distancing

Seeing the above simulation, it is absolutely clear that even with a small 8% of the population not obeying the lockdown, the virus continues to spread for a long periods of time, sometimes even infecting 50% of the population. Hence, not violating social distancing is crucial to limiting the spread of the virus.
