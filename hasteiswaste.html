Haste Makes Waste

"Early bird catches the worm,
but the early worm gets caught."

In the strict social distancing model, people isolated themselves until no one was infected anymore. However, if lockdowns were lifted before everyone had recovered, this may not be the case. In the model below, lockdowns are lifted automatically once only 4% of the population is infected.

Lockdowns lifted early

As you can see, even if a tiny percentage of the population has contracted the virus while the lockdown is lifted, the situation gradually evolves into a zero-lockdown model. Therefore, social distances must be maintained even after the lockdowns are lifted.
